Rock, Paper, Scissors Game

Game Rules:

Press the buttont to start. Click or tap the rock, paper or scissor images to make your selection. The computer will randomly generate a selection.

Rock defeats scissors. Paper defeats rock. Scissors defeats paper. The same selection results in a tie.

Your score is displayed below alongside your rock, paper or scissor selections.

Good luck!
